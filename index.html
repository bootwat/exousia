Welcome

to Exousia Door Evangel Ministry (E.D.E.M)

Established and founded on the 27th of February, 1999, by The Desert Seer, Prophet Elijah Dankano, E.D.E.M is an interdenominational ministry, and has grown after time, and is still growing strong as the Prophet has taken some selected under his wing, Pastors and Pastoresses and so many that are too many to mention, his drive has never died down from the day he started till date, and it's still burning strong. With branches in Abuja, Gombe, and still coming to different states in the country, and hopefully internationally, E.D.E.M is a ministry drived towards the achievement of the riches of the kingdom of God and bring the children of God back to the father, and the lost to the saving knowlegde of grace, of our Lord and Saviour, Jesus Christ.
