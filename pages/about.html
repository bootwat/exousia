About

Exousia Door Evangel Ministry (E.D.E.M)

Established and founded on the 27th of February, 1999, by The Desert Seer, Prophet Elijah Dankano, E.D.E.M is an interdenominational ministry, and grown after time, and is still growing strong as the Prophet has taken some selected under his wing, Pastors and Prophetesses and so many that are too many to mention, his drive has never died down from the day he started till date, and it's still burning strong. With branches in Abuja, Gombe, and still coming to different states in the country, and hopefully internationally, E.D.E.M is a drived towards the achievement of the riches of the kingdom and bring the children of God back to the father, and the lost to the saving knowlegde of our Lord and Saviour, Jesus Christ.

Our Goals

at E.D.E.M, with Prophet & Prophetess Elijah Dankano

1. Preach the Gospel

Our goal of preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ, is the most important and dear to our hearts, it is one of the biggest given to all believer by God, and it is paramount to see it done, to bring the Lord's children closer to the Father, and bring the lost to his saving knowlegde of Grace.

2. Heal

God has not given us a spirit of sickness or fear of darkness, and at E.D.E.M we hold fast the power that has given freely, that we should not fear the devil, who will try as hard as possible to bring you down with sickness, and tourment, at E.D.E.M, see God raise you up from the stronghold and set you free, for the Father has set free is free indeed.

3. Praise

We shall come into his presense with singing and dancing, because he is worthy to be praised, at E.D.E.M, we love praise the Lord, we love singing of his glory, and praise his Holy Name, we lift up our hands and voices to sing. We stump our feets and dance, as we bring down every wall of the devil, just as the children of God did at the wall of Jericho.

4. Raising Kingdom Stars

With a generation of youth, teenagers and even children, we see a big responsibility to bring up and nuture this youth to the knowlegde of God, to raise children of Kingdom (Kingdom Stars). The evil has made a mission to attack these young ones and destroys their life, at E.D.E.M we've stumped our against this, and made a priority to change the tide and fulfil this mission.
